Creating Users:

use: sysync --adduser=foo

edit the user:
sysync --edituser=foo

You'll see, in your favorite text editor:
username: foo
uid: 1001
fullname: Foo Bar
homedir: /home/foo
shell: /bin/bash
disabled: 0
ssh_keys:
   - "sshkey one goes here"
   - "sshkey two goes here"


Now, set an initial password:

sysync --passwd=foo

Enter the initial password.
